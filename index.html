Frequently Asked Questions

I gathered some frequently asked questions from clients
Check it out.

  • How much does a website cost?

    The cost of a website can vary depending on various factors, just like the cost of a house may vary. The negotiation will always depend on the type of website you want, the contents you have, your personal budget range and your unique needs. My websites are very affordable.

  • How much does an app cost?

    Apps in general are more expensive than websites.
    The cost will of course vary for different kinds as they all require lots of coding to achieve. Another factor for their expense are the specs you want in your app.

  • How does the payment process work?

    The project starts with a 50% deposit and the second installment comes after completion of the project.

  • How long does it take to build a website?

    My Standard websites does not exceed 60 days while E-commerce takes maximum of 90 days to create. The time will vary from project to project.

  • How long does it take to build an app?

    A smaller app will take shorter time compared to a bigger app.
    It always depends on the kind of app you want to be developed (web apps, mobile apps, desktop apps etc.) Apps takes months because they go through phases and have to be properly tested before deployment. It will usually take 3-4 months for me to successfully develop a mobile app that is ready for public release, 2-3 months for web apps and 4-6 months for desktop apps. Please note that this does not include the product definition or design stages of building the apps.

  • Can you help me update my existing website that someone else built?

    YES, i offer updating services.

  • Can you help me with content for my website?

    YES, i create contents for clients that don't have, i build sitemaps and help structure the foundation of your website. Content Creation is very important in web designing.

  • Can you help me create content for my app?

    No, i don't offer that service. You must have your contents before i develop an app for you.
